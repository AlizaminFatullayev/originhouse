THE PRODUCT STORY

Yirgacheffe is synonymous with the finest floral-noted coffees in the world. Our journey to find this specific lot took us through the rolling hills of the Gedeo zone, where we partnered with a washing station committed to regenerative agricultural practices.

Every bean is hand-sorted and fermented for 36 hours before being slow-dried on raised African beds. This painstaking process preserves the delicate organic acids that give this coffee its signature tea-like clarity and stone fruit sweetness.

About Our Coffee House

A legacy of quiet ritual.

Origin House began with a singular, quiet intention: to reclaim the ritual of coffee from the rush of modern life. We believe that a cup of coffee is more than a caffeine delivery system—it is a moment of stillness, a testament to craftsmanship, and a bridge between the farmer and the connoisseur.

Our journey started in a small, wood-paneled space where we spent months perfecting our signature roasts. We didn't just want coffee that tasted good; we wanted coffee that told a story of the high-altitude volcanic soils of Ethiopia and the mist-covered mountains of Colombia.

Today, Origin House serves as a sanctuary for those who appreciate the finer details. From the weight of our custom-thrown ceramic mugs to the precise temperature of our water, every element is curated to honor the bean and the person drinking it.

Our Pillar

The Philosophy of Origin

Quality Beans

We source only Specialty Grade beans, ensuring each crop is harvested at the peak of ripeness and processed with absolute precision.

Thoughtful Design

Our spaces are designed as extensions of the coffee experience—minimalist, quiet, and intentional to let your senses focus on the cup.

Local Experience

Each location honors its neighborhood, integrating local art and culture into the refined world of Origin House.

Ethics & Earth

Responsibility from Root to Cup

Sustainability is not a marketing term for us; it is a prerequisite. We partner directly with farmers to ensure above-Fair-Trade wages, and our packaging is 100% compostable, designed to return to the earth without a trace.
